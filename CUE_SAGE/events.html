SAGE Events & Activities

Discover our comprehensive range of events and activities designed to promote sustainability and environmental awareness across our community.

Workshops and Training

Sustainable Living Workshop

Learn practical sustainable living practices including waste reduction, energy conservation, and eco-friendly product usage. Interactive sessions with hands-on activities and take-home guides.

3 hours 20-30 people

Renewable Energy Training

Comprehensive training on renewable energy sources including solar and wind power. Learn practical applications and participate in hands-on demonstrations.

4 hours 15-25 people

Eco-Friendly Gardening Workshop

Master eco-friendly gardening practices including composting, permaculture, and organic farming. Perfect for beginners and experienced gardeners alike.

2.5 hours 25-35 people

Environmental Events

Tree Planting Day

Join us for our annual tree planting event to promote reforestation and create green spaces. Each participant plants 3-5 trees and learns about local ecosystems.

Full day 50+ people

Beach & Park Clean-up

Participate in our regular clean-up events to raise awareness about waste management and pollution. Includes educational sessions on recycling and environmental impact.

3-4 hours 30-50 people

Environmental Film Screening

Screen documentaries on environmental issues followed by interactive discussions. Educational and thought-provoking sessions for all ages.

2.5 hours 40-60 people

Awareness Campaigns

Climate Change Awareness Campaign

Launch campaigns to raise awareness about climate change impacts and mitigation strategies. Includes workshops, social media campaigns, and community outreach.

1 week 100+ people

Sustainable Transportation Campaign

Promote sustainable transportation options including cycling, walking, and public transport. Includes bike-to-work challenges and awareness drives.
